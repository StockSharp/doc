# JMA

**Jurikâ€™s Moving Average (JMA)** is an indicator that is a variation of the moving average. The curve of the indicator is characterized by good smoothing, as well as minimal lead time after the end of price movements and minimal lag from strong price movements.

To use the indicator, the [JurikMovingAverage](xref:StockSharp.Algo.Indicators.JurikMovingAverage) class should be used.

##### Indicator Settings.

- Phase - determines the speed at which the moving average will unfold;
- Period - determines the period of the moving average.
